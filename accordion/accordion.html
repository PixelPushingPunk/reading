Foundation in Science

Start your journey to University of Reading Malaysia Science Degree

International recognition for research-led teaching, a dynamic environment and an excellent employment record for graduates make the University of Reading Malaysia your ideal destination for a science degree.

Our Foundation in Science programme will certainly help you realise your dreams. As a one-year intensive access course for students without British A-Levels, the programme will prepare you academically and socially to start an undergraduate science programme. Upon successful completion, your progression to University of Reading Malaysia is guaranteed.

This foundation programme covers all the elements you need - a background in science, life skills and transferable Academic Skills. It is designed to prepare you for a rewarding university experience.

With a wealth of science courses, expert faculty members and world-class facilities, your University of Reading Malaysia science degree is our ticket towards an exciting and lucrative career. Let our Foundation in Science programme get you on the right path.
